# AI Bias Engine

The AI bias engine is the decision layer that summarizes market state into a tradable regime.

## Bias states

SwiftChart can mark the market as bullish, bearish, balanced, transition, breakdown, breakout, or no-trade.

## Faster regime changes

The engine can switch from bullish to bearish faster when structural support breaks and lower-high/lower-low behavior appears with bearish momentum.

## Guardrails

Small pullbacks do not automatically flip the bias. The engine requires structure plus confirmation before changing global posture.
