# Smart Entry Conditions

Smart entry conditions decide where a setup is allowed to form.

## Long entries

Longs prefer support reactions, bullish sweeps, reclaimed resistance, or confirmed bullish transition structure.

## Short entries

Shorts prefer resistance rejection, breakdown retest, failed reclaim, bearish sweep, or structural support break with momentum.

## Quality control

If price is too far from a useful invalidation point or risk/reward is poor, SwiftChart rejects the setup even when direction is correct.
