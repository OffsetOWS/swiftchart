# Liquidity + Volatility Logic

Liquidity and volatility help SwiftChart decide whether a move is meaningful or just noise.

## Liquidity sweeps

A sweep happens when price raids a recent high or low, then reclaims or rejects the level. Confirmed sweeps can strengthen reversal setups.

## Volatility checks

The scanner avoids dead markets and overly compressed ranges unless a confirmed structural break is already active.

## Combined reading

The best setups often combine a liquidity event, directional displacement, and a clean invalidation point.
