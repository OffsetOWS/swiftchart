# FAQ

SwiftChart is built around bias, confirmation, and risk. These answers cover the common questions traders ask before using the scanner.

## Does SwiftChart trade for me?

No. SwiftChart currently focuses on analysis, trade ideas, alerts, and saved history. Execution features are designed to stay behind explicit safety controls.

## Why are there no setups sometimes?

No setup is a valid output. The engine blocks trades when price is mid-range, conditions are choppy, risk/reward is weak, or bias confirmation is incomplete.

## Why did a long disappear?

Longs are reduced or disabled when bearish structure is active. Price needs to reclaim structure or form a high-quality reversal before long setups become eligible again.

## Why did a short not trigger?

Shorts need bearish context plus a usable entry trigger. A rejection, failed reclaim, support break, bearish retest, or sweep must line up with momentum and risk/reward.

## Is Telegram required?

No. Telegram is used for alert delivery. The dashboard and docs work independently.
