# What is SwiftChart?

SwiftChart is a market-structure assistant for traders who want cleaner context before taking a setup. It reads price action, support and resistance, liquidity behavior, trend pressure, and recent signal history, then turns those inputs into a focused bias and setup view.

The goal is not to predict every candle. The goal is to reduce noisy decision making. SwiftChart highlights when the market is trending, ranging, breaking down, breaking out, or too messy to trade.

## Core idea

SwiftChart separates market context from trade execution. First it asks whether the environment is worth trading. Then it checks whether long or short conditions have enough confirmation. Only after those filters pass does it produce a trade idea.

## What it watches

- Market bias and regime shifts
- Structural support and resistance
- Liquidity sweeps and failed reclaims
- Momentum and EMA confirmation
- Risk/reward and invalidation levels
- Signal history and review outcomes

## What it is not

SwiftChart is not a guaranteed profit system. It is a structured workflow for reading market conditions with consistent rules. Every output should still be reviewed with risk controls and personal discretion.
