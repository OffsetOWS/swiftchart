# Quick Start

Use this flow when opening SwiftChart for a fresh market read.

## Step 1: Launch the app

Click **Launch App** from the homepage. SwiftChart initializes the market scanner, checks market structure, and prepares the dashboard.

## Step 2: Review bias

Start with the market regime and bias cards. If the engine marks the market as choppy, wait. If it marks a trend or transition, check which side is favored.

## Step 3: Check top ideas

The top ideas list ranks setups that pass structure, confirmation, and risk filters. Each idea includes entry, stop, targets, confidence, and invalidation.

## Step 4: Save or monitor

Use the dashboard to review signals and history. Use Telegram alerts when you want SwiftChart to notify you when the scanner finds clean conditions.
