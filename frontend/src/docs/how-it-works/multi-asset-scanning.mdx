# Multi-Asset Scanning

SwiftChart scans liquid Hyperliquid markets and ranks the cleanest opportunities. The scanner is designed to avoid spending attention on every coin equally.

## Discovery

Markets are discovered from Hyperliquid, normalized into SwiftChart symbols, and filtered for active trading.

## Prefiltering

Before full analysis, the scanner checks whether a market has enough movement, volume, and distance from mid-range.

## Ranking

Valid setups are ranked by setup score, risk/reward, zone quality, and signal strength.
