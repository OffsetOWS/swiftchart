# Risk Management

Risk management keeps every trade idea grounded. SwiftChart does not accept an idea just because direction looks right.

## Risk/reward

Every setup must pass the minimum risk/reward requirement. If the stop is too wide or targets are too close, the idea is rejected.

## Position context

SwiftChart calculates entry, stop, target, and invalidation from the same structural map used by the signal engine.

## Invalidation

Every idea includes a simple invalidation statement. If price closes beyond the invalidation level, the setup should be considered broken.
