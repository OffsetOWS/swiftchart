# Market Bias Detection

SwiftChart bias detection reads structural breaks, swing behavior, EMA pressure, and momentum together. A single pullback is not enough to flip the global bias.

## Bullish bias

Bullish bias requires higher-high or higher-low behavior, reclaimed resistance, and positive momentum confirmation.

## Bearish bias

Bearish bias activates when price breaks recent structural support, forms lower-high and lower-low behavior, and confirms downside with EMA or momentum pressure.

## Transitions

Transition regimes allow SwiftChart to react faster without flipping on every small candle. The engine waits for multiple confirmations before accepting a trade in the new direction.
